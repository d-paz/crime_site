How it works

This website utilizes the Crime Data Explorer (CDE) API provided by the Federal Beureau of Information. This freely available resource allows law enforcement and civilians to gain valuable information regarding violent and property crimes.

Please be aware that the data provided may not be completely up to date. There is typically a delay from the date of offence to the date the CDE reports it.
